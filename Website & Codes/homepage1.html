This isn’t just survival and escape. It’s a fight against your own mind swallowed by fear. Every corner hides a twisted equation. Every wrong answer brings them closer.

You lost your one closest companion, and the world hasn’t felt the same. One night, you drifted into sleep due to overwhelmed and grief.

Now you wake on the rooftop of St. Michael Academy. The halls below are silent, air thick rising, and bearing the darkness while there is someone standing beside, which is your friend you thought was gone. As you step into the shadowed hallways to do escape, the school begins to twist. It feels eerie, familiar faces feel strange. The janitor, the teacher, the guard are waiting, watching, and chasing.

But what stands isn’t just fear—it’s the math. Equations that drag you deeper, numbers that whisper, and logic that will make you suffer, forcing you to think and solve under pressure for you to forward.
